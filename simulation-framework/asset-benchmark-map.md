# Asset and Benchmark Map

## Crypto regulatory / sentiment cases
Primary assets:
- BTC
- ETH
- major exchange-linked assets or equities
- broad crypto beta basket

Benchmarks:
- BTC/ETH pair comparison
- broad crypto market index or basket
- exchange-linked names versus BTC

## Commodity / energy supply shock cases
Primary assets:
- front-month commodity future
- producers / miners / energy equities

Benchmarks:
- broad commodity index
- relevant sector ETF/basket
- broad equity index

## Monetary policy cases
Primary assets:
- front-end and belly rates proxies
- sovereign bonds
- FX pair linked to the region
- banks / rate-sensitive equities

Benchmarks:
- broad equity index
- regional FX basket
- rates curve benchmark

## Regulatory regime cases
Primary assets:
- exchanges or platforms most exposed to policy change
- major assets affected by valuation rerating

Benchmarks:
- broad sector beta
- market leader asset
- regulatory-insensitive control basket
