# Decision Ladder

## Levels
- Ignore
- Archive
- Watch
- Research
- Simulate
- Paper Trade Candidate
- Manual Review Required

## Interpretation
- Ignore -> no action value
- Archive -> keep as context, no active follow-up
- Watch -> monitor for confirmation or escalation
- Research -> worth structured investigation
- Simulate -> strong enough for model testing / paper logic
- Paper Trade Candidate -> only after validation and guardrails
- Manual Review Required -> needs human judgment before further action
